UNESCO World Heritage Knowledge Graph

A five-star, FAIR-oriented RDF knowledge graph of the UNESCO World Heritage sites, derived from the official World Heritage Centre syndication export.

Persistent namespace: https://w3id.org/whs/. Each site is identified as https://w3id.org/whs/site/{id_no} (e.g., site/755) and each danger-listing event as https://w3id.org/whs/danger/{id_no}-{n}. States and regions are referenced by their Getty TGN and UNESCO Thesaurus URIs.
